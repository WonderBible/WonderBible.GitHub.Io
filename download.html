WonderBible Desktop App

1st, You MUST Be On Microsoft Edge. Then Tap The Logo & It Will Take You To WonderBible's HomePage

2nd, Tap The Settings And More Button

3rd, Hover To Apps

4th, And Tap "Install this site as an app"

5th, Tap The Install Button

6th, And Finally, Select Which Effect Should The App Made And Tap Allow. (If You Want Nothing, Just Tap Don't Allow). And Your WonderBible Desktop App Is Downloaded :)
